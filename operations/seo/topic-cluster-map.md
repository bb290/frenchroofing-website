## Interlinking rules (the part most sites skip)

1. Every supporting piece links **up** to its pillar (contextual, in-body).
2. Every pillar links **down** to all its supporting pieces and **across** to its money page(s).
3. Supporting pieces link to the money page only where natural (one CTA block + at most one in-body link).
4. Clusters cross-link only at genuine seams (e.g., Leak Guide → Replacement Decision for "when a leak means replacement").
5. City pages link to the pillar relevant to their service (e.g., roof-cleaning city pages → Moss Handbook).

## AEO formatting standard (applies to every piece)

- H1 = the question/topic in natural language. H2s = the actual long-tail questions.
- First 2–3 sentences under each H2 = a complete, citable answer (this is what AI tools lift).
- FAQ schema on every piece (already automatic via `FAQSection`), `Article` schema on posts.
- Real photos from the job-site library with descriptive alts — never stock.
- One relevant CTA per piece, matched to the persona's moment (urgent → phone; planner → instant estimate; mover → free inspection).
