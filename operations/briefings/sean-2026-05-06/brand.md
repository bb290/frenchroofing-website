# French Roofing Brand Guide

**For:** Sean
**A short reference to what French Roofing sounds like, looks like, and stands for online.**

## How we sound

- **Down to earth.** Real sentences. No marketing slogans.
- **Neighborly.** Like talking with someone over the fence.
- **Light hearted.** A joke when the moment fits. Dry humor. Self-aware.
- **Never salesy.** Quality and accountability over price.

## How we don't sound

- We don't say "premier roofing services."
- We don't say "industry-leading," "world-class," or "best-in-class."
- We don't say "rockstar," "amazing team," or "synergy."
- We don't use long dashes (the em dash punctuation).
- We don't use hashtags on Instagram.
- We don't write the way roofing competitors write. If a post sounds like it could come from any other roofer's account, we rewrite it.

## What we always say

- **Tagline: "Got You Covered!"** Used sparingly, maybe one out of every six posts. Better to have it land than to wear it out.
- **Call to action:** "Visit website for Instant Roof Quote / Link in Bio." Same wording every time. Sends people to the quote tool on the website.
- **Identity:** French Roofing, family-owned, Damascus, Oregon, since 2014. CertainTeed certified.

## Look and feel

- **Colors:** yellow, orange, blue, grey. Yellow and orange come through as accents. Blue or grey usually anchors the bottom of an Instagram post.
- **Font:** Inter. Clean, modern, easy to read on a phone.
- **Photos:** real French Roofing crews on real Pacific Northwest homes in natural light. No stock photos. No AI-generated images. No filters.
- **Logo:** placed in the corner of every Instagram post (currently bottom-left).
- **Aesthetic:** Californian surfer vibe meets Pacific Northwest contractor. Laid back. Confident without flexing.

## The story we tell

- **Sean.** Twenty years on roofs. Started as a subcontractor at sixteen. Founded French Roofing in 2014. Family-owned and operated. Still on roofs some days, mostly running the business now.
- **Crew & Community.** Real people doing the work. Customers and neighbors. Damascus, Happy Valley, Clackamas.
- **Workmanship.** Manufacturer specs, clean job sites, written warranties. Honest delivery of bad news with honest options.
- **The grandma rule.** Every roof gets built the way you'd build it for your own grandma.

## Three things we never claim

- "Cheapest in the area." We don't compete on price.
- "Premier" or "industry-leading" anything. Empty words.
- High-pressure scarcity tactics ("today only," "don't wait"). Never.

## Service area

Twenty miles around Damascus, Oregon. Local content references only cities in that radius — Damascus, Clackamas, Happy Valley first, then Gresham, Oregon City, Milwaukie, Lake Oswego, Tualatin, West Linn, Sandy, Estacada, Boring, Troutdale, Fairview, Wood Village, Gladstone, Johnson City, Canby, and SE Portland neighborhoods.
