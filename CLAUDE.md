## Brand Story

### How It Started
Sean French grew up on roofs. He started as a subcontractor at sixteen, learning the trade the way most real craftspeople do; By showing up early, listening more than talking, and doing the unglamorous work with pride.

In 2014, he started French Roofing on the simple idea: Do the work like you're doing it for your own grandma.

"I've just believe every customer's roof should be built the same way you'd build it for your own grandma," Sean says.

From then to now: Sean on a ladder, equipped with a toolbelt and a dream has become a fully trained, safety-focused team committed to high-quality roofing solutions.

### Values You Can See on the Roof
At French Roofing, values aren't posters on the wall. They are core beliefs built into the habits practiced on every job, from small roof repair projects to full roof replacement.

We've grown since that first job, but our approach is still hands-on, skilled craftsmanship, and rooted in community.

- **Integrity** means we communicate clearly and honestly, even when something unexpected comes up. If weather delays a step or we find hidden damage, you hear from us first, with a plan and honest options. We stand behind our word and do what's right for the customer as our guiding principle.
- **Workmanship** shows up in the details you can see and the ones you can't. We maintain clean, safe job sites, follow manufacturer specs, document every stage, and backed with a standard workmanship warranty.
- **Community** is both our neighborhood and our responsibility. We take care around gardens, siding, and shared spaces. We protect homes during storms, help neighbors navigate insurance, and treat every property like it's owned by our grandma.

### A Local Team Focused on Homeowners
French Roofing has grown steadily in Clackamas and the surrounding area by staying rooted in the same simple purpose: keep our families warm, dry, and safe.

Over the years, Sean has built a team that takes pride in doing things the right way. The company invests in certifications, crew training, material education, inspections, and quality checks that meet code without drama or excuses. Warranties are honored without fine-print games. Communication stays honest, even when the news is inconvenient. And every job ends with a site that's cleaner than when we arrived.

### Quiet Credibility
Today, French Roofing is known for roofs that hold up, jobs that stay on schedule, and a team that treats homeowners with respect. The work is straightforward, the promises are kept, and the craftsmanship speaks for itself.

## AI Workstreams

### 1. SOP Management
- All SOPs live in GetOutline (or equivalent knowledge base)
- SOPs must follow a consistent, AI-searchable format: Title, Purpose, Scope, Trigger, Steps, Owner, Last Updated
- Content must be preserved exactly when reformatting — no paraphrasing or summarizing SOP logic
- Priority SOPs to build first: estimating workflow, job handoff, insurance claim process, customer follow-up, subcontractor onboarding

### 2. SEO / AEO Optimization
- Local SEO is the primary channel — target high-intent roofing queries by city
- Build city landing pages starting with 3 priority cities, then expand
- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) — structure content to be cited by AI search tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE)
- Target keyword patterns: `[service] in [city] OR`, `roofing contractor [city]`, `roof replacement cost [city]`
- Every page must include: LocalBusiness schema markup, NAP consistency, service-specific copy, and the primary CTA

### 3. Content Factory
- Repurpose core blog/service content into: social media posts, video scripts, AEO answer blocks, email sequences
- Content pillars: storm damage education, roofing materials comparison, maintenance tips, local authority content
- Maintain brand voice across all formats (down to earth, neighborly, light hearted)
- All content must reference Damascus, OR as home base and reinforce the service area
