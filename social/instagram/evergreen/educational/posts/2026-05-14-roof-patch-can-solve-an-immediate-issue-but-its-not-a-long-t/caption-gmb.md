# Caption (gmb)

Roof patch vs. roof replacement, Damascus and Clackamas area homeowners ask us this all the time.

A patch is the right call for isolated damage, one lifted tab after a windstorm, a single compromised vent boot. It stops the problem and buys real time when the rest of the roof is still in good shape.

But a patch over failed flashing, cracked underlayment, or shingles with heavy granule loss is just delaying a bigger repair. We see it on roofs all over the Damascus, Happy Valley, and Clackamas area, a band-aid that held for a couple of years and then let water in somewhere new.

We'll always tell you honestly which situation you're in. If a patch is enough, we'll say so. If it isn't, we'll explain why and give you real options.
