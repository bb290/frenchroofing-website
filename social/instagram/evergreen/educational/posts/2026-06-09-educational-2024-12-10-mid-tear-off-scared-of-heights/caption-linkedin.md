# Caption (linkedin)

Mid-tear-off is one of the most important stages of a roof replacement, and one of the least talked about.

Once the old shingles are gone, the deck is exposed and we can actually see what we're working with. Soft spots, rotted sheathing, popped nails, improper old repairs. These are the things that don't show up in a street-level inspection and don't show up in photos. They show up when the old roof comes off.

For property managers and GCs, this matters. It's the stage where scope can change, and where a crew that communicates clearly earns its keep. We document what we find, walk the owner through the options, and don't just cover it back up and hope for the best.

French Roofing has been doing this work in Damascus and the surrounding communities since 2014, CCB #203933, CertainTeed Certified. We've seen a lot of decks that had surprises. The goal is always no surprises for you.
