# Caption (gmb)

Roof drip edge installation in Damascus, Clackamas, and Happy Valley, OR.

The drip edge is a small metal strip installed along the eave and rake of a roof. It seems minor, but it keeps water from rolling back under the shingle edge and soaking into the fascia and roof deck. When it's missing or installed out of order, that moisture has nowhere to go except into the wood.

We install drip edge on every replacement we do, in the correct sequence: under the underlayment at the rakes, over it at the eaves. It's a small detail that protects a lot of wood over the life of the roof.
