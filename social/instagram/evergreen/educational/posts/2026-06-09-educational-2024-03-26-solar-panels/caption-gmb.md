# Caption (gmb)

Thinking about solar panels in Damascus, Clackamas, or Happy Valley? Get your roof assessed first.

When solar panels are mounted, installers drill through the roof deck and set flashings around each penetration. If those flashings aren't properly seated or the wrong sealant is used, it can lead to slow leaks that don't show up until real damage is done inside.

We recommend a roof inspection before any panel installation. Know what condition your roof is in, and make sure any existing issues are addressed before something gets bolted on top.

If panels are already up and you're seeing moisture or ceiling stains, the penetration flashings are the first place we look.
