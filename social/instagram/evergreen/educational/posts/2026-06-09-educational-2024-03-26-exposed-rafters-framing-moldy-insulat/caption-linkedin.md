# Caption (linkedin)

This is what deferred roof maintenance can look like from the inside: exposed rafters, rotting framing, and mold-saturated insulation.

By the time insulation looks like this, the water intrusion isn't recent. Mold growth in a roof cavity typically means moisture has been present for weeks or months, not days. And in many cases, the ceiling below still looks completely normal.

For property managers and building owners, that's the part worth knowing. Visible interior damage is usually a late indicator. The earlier signals, granule loss in gutters, soft spots on ceilings, a persistent musty smell in upper rooms, are easier and cheaper to act on.
