Roof installation done right in Damascus, OR starts well before the first shingle goes down.

This photo shows a shingle installation mid-progress, and there's more going on here than it looks. Bundles are staged across the deck to distribute weight evenly. Courses are planned out before work begins so the pattern is consistent all the way to the ridge. Starter strip is set first because that bottom edge takes the most wind load.

The details in this stage are what separate a roof that lasts from one that causes problems a few years in.
