# Caption (linkedin)

When we pull off an old roof, the first thing we do before touching a shingle bundle is look at the deck.

Soft spots, rotted sheathing, cracked boards, any of that gets replaced before the new roof system goes down. In the Damascus and Clackamas area, we find moisture-damaged decking on a regular basis, especially on roofs that ran a few seasons past their prime. The PNW climate is patient about working water into places you can't see from the ground.

For property managers and GCs, this is the step that separates a roof replacement that lasts 30 years from one that causes callbacks in five. It's not an upsell. It's the foundation the whole system sits on.

We've been doing this for 10+ years, licensed and insured (CCB #203933), CertainTeed Certified. When we find something that needs attention under the old roof, you hear about it with options, not surprises.
