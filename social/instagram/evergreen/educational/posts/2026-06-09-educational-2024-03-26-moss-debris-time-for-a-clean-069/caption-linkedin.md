# Caption (linkedin)

Moss and debris are some of the quietest sources of roof damage we see on homes in Damascus, Clackamas, and Happy Valley.

Moss holds moisture directly against the shingles. Over time that constant dampness breaks down the granule surface, softens the mat, and creates a pathway for water to work under the tabs. Debris in the valleys, leaves, pine needles, branches, does the same thing by trapping standing water where it should be draining freely.

For property managers and homeowners looking to protect a long-term asset, a roof cleaning is one of the better-value maintenance investments out there. We clear the moss and debris, inspect the valleys and low spots while we're on the roof, and give you a straight report on what we found.
