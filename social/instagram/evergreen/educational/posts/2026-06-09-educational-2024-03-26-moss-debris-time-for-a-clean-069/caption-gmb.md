# Caption (gmb)

Moss and debris building up on your roof in Damascus, Clackamas, or Happy Valley? It's worth addressing before the heavy rain season sets in.

Moss holds moisture against the shingles, wearing down the granule surface and softening the mat underneath. Debris in the valleys, leaves, pine needles, branches, traps water where it should be draining fast. Over time both cause real damage that a simple cleaning could have prevented.
