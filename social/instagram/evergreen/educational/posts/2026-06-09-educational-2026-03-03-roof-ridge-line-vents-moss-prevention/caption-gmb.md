# Caption (gmb)

Roof ridge vents and moss prevention in Damascus, OR.

Ridge vents do more than cool your attic in summer. They keep the roof deck dry in winter, and that's where moss prevention really starts.

Without proper ventilation, warm moist air from inside the house condenses against a cold roof deck. That moisture feeds moss and algae, and breaks down the sheathing over time. Homes in the Damascus, Clackamas, and Happy Valley area with poor ridge ventilation almost always show heavier moss growth near the peak, right where airflow is weakest.

If you're seeing moss creeping toward the ridge line, a ventilation check should be part of the conversation, not just a clean and treat.
