# Caption (linkedin)

Full roof deck replacement in progress, and it's a good reminder of what's actually under every roof we install.

The deck is the structural layer everything else gets nailed to. When we pull old shingles and find soft spots, rot, or delaminated plywood, we replace the bad sections before any underlayment or shingles go down. Cutting corners here shows up later, sometimes in the form of a warranty claim, sometimes as a callback that didn't have to happen.

In the Damascus and Clackamas area, moisture is the usual culprit. Failed flashing, worn vent boots, aging valley seams. The damage is quiet until it isn't.

For property managers and GCs vetting roofing subs, this is one of the questions worth asking on any job: what did the deck look like, and what did you do about it? A contractor who documents it and shows you is one worth keeping around. French Roofing has been doing this work since 2014, CCB #203933, CertainTeed Certified.
