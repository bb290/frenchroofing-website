Roof deck repairs in Damascus, Clackamas, and Happy Valley.

When we tear off old shingles, the wood decking underneath tells us a lot. In the Pacific Northwest, moisture has a way of finding small gaps over the years, and by the time a roof is due for replacement, sections of the deck are sometimes soft or rotted.

We replace compromised decking before any new materials go down. A shingle is only as good as what it's nailed into, and skipping that step leads to fastener failure and a shorter roof life.
